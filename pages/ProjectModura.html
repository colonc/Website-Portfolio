MODURA - eCommerce Protoype

Mock-up, color palette, and logo designed for Modura, an e-Commerce clothing brand designed for trendy men and women between 18 - 25 years old. These individuals are early in their careers and are looking for an affordable solution for business casual clothing to wear to their jobs in urban areas. They are fashion-forward, and conscious of trends. They want to dress for the jobs they want without appearing overdressed.
