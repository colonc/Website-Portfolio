Rethink Your Assumptions - Learn German/Deutsch

Built 6 bi-lingual posters surrounding the topic of questioning one’s assumption/preconceived notions regarding the German culture and identity. Designed to be hung either “right side up” or “upside down”, the posters invited viewers to flip one’s assumptions on their head, and brought to light the richness and diversity of German culture.
